Good to know

Frequently asked

Is powerwashing safe for my siding?

Yes — we use lower, surface-safe pressure (often called soft washing) for siding and wood so it cleans thoroughly without damage.

What can you powerwash?

Driveways, walkways, patios, decks, fences, and most siding. If you're unsure about a surface, just ask.

Will powerwashing remove oil stains from my driveway?

We can significantly reduce most stains. Deep, old oil stains may not vanish entirely, but the surface comes back dramatically cleaner.
